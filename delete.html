Delete Your Data

You have complete control over your data in ER Time. This guide will help you delete all your information from our systems.

Important Notice

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your data, you will lose all your reviews, ratings, and app preferences. You will need to set up the app again if you decide to use it in the future.

Method 1: Delete Data Through the App

This is the easiest way to delete your data:

1

Open ER Time App

Launch the ER Time app on your device

2

Go to Settings

Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Settings"

3

Find Privacy Section

Scroll down and look for "Privacy & Data" or "Account Settings"

4

Select Delete All Data

Tap "Delete All My Data" or "Remove Account"

5

Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message and tap "Confirm Delete" to proceed

Method 2: Email Request

If you can't access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Email Instructions

Send an email to deletion@ertime.app with the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your device ID (found in app settings)
  • Approximate date you started using the app
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted
Send Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your data, we remove:

Location Data

All location history and search queries

Reviews and Ratings

All your hospital reviews, ratings, and feedback

Wait Time Reports

All wait time submissions and reports

App Preferences

Settings, preferences, and app configuration

Usage Analytics

Anonymous usage data associated with your device

What Happens After Deletion

Immediate Actions

  • Your data is marked for deletion immediately
  • You will receive a confirmation email
  • App will log you out and reset to initial state

Complete Removal

  • Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are also removed
  • You will receive final confirmation email

Partial Data Deletion

If you only want to delete specific data:

Delete Individual Reviews

Go to your profile → My Reviews → Select review → Delete

Clear Location History

Settings → Privacy → Clear Location Data

Reset App Data

Settings → Privacy → Reset App Data (keeps account)

Data Recovery

No Recovery Available

Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. If you change your mind, you will need to start fresh with the app. We recommend downloading your data before deletion if you think you might want it later.
