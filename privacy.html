Overview

ER Wait Time ("we," "our," or "us") helps you find nearby hospitals and estimated emergency department wait times. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices—including for our optional premium subscription.

Information we collect

  • Location — when you search for hospitals (to find nearby facilities and distances).
  • Account — if you register: email/username and credentials you provide.
  • Reviews & wait-time reports — ratings, comments, and visit-related info you submit.
  • Usage & diagnostics — anonymous analytics, crash logs, and device/OS type to improve the app.
  • Subscriptions — purchase status via Apple App Store or Google Play (we do not store full payment card data).

Information we do not collect

  • Full medical records or clinical diagnoses
  • Payment card numbers (handled by Apple/Google)
  • Data from other apps on your device without your action

Premium subscription

ER Wait Time Premium (er_premium_monthly) is an auto-renewing monthly subscription at $3.99/month. After 30 free hospital searches, a subscription is required to continue searching. Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google Play account. You can cancel anytime in your store account settings.

Terms of Use (EULA) · Manage subscription (Apple) · Manage subscription (Google)

How we use information

  • Provide hospital search, wait times, maps, and directions
  • Operate accounts, subscriptions, and restore purchases
  • Improve predictions, performance, and security
  • Respond to support and legal/data-rights requests

We do not sell your personal data.

Data security & retention

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and secure hosting. We retain data only as long as needed for the service or as required by law. You may request deletion or export at any time (see below).

Your data rights

You may request access, export, correction, or deletion of your data, or withdraw consent.

  • Privacy Policy (full)
  • Terms of Service / Terms of Use (EULA)
  • Request account deletion
  • Request data export

In the app: Settings → Subscription → Data Rights, or Profile to delete your account when signed in.

Third-party services

  • Google Maps / location services
  • Google AdMob (ads for non-premium users)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (subscriptions)
  • Backend API at api.mywaitime.com

Each provider has its own privacy terms.

Children

ER Wait Time is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
